Why does Reach and Relevance matter?

Reach and Relevance is central to our client impact because it helps us:

Develop partners who are “trusted peers” of influential leaders
Creates “good will” with our communities
Stimulates client discussions and supports clientele development efforts
Improves our relevance & impact in client service
