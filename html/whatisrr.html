What is R&R?

Peter Dahlstrom
Senior Partner, Client and Firm Communications

Reach and Relevance is critically important to our mission of delivering lasting client impact. Through greater “reach,” we connect with an ever expanding network of clients, potential clients, and other influencers. Through greater “relevance,” we bring value to that network by sharing distinctive knowledge and insights into their business, economic and societal context.

Leverage this website to build a case for R&R, identify your support network, browse through inspirational examples of R&R from others at the firm, find tools and resources, and get guidance on how to design and implement a Reach and Relevance journey.

A few things to keep in mind as you get started:

Partners will prioritize R&R differently. For some people R&R is a big priority. For some, it’s an enabler of other priorities. While others still are just getting started in their R&R journey. Not everyone has to have the same goals or path.

A successful R&R strategy doesn't happen overnight. As you get started, you might aspire to creating a strong foundation – getting to a baseline level of R&R. Remember, it’s the small persistent steps that will take you where you want to go. Setting achievable goals along the way will help build necessary momentum to make lasting impact.

Reach and Relevance is: Following a strategic approach in developing and achieving a vision, leveraging a mix of channels over time, and evaluating and adjusting performance over time

Reach and Relevance is not: Non-strategic, ad hoc external reputation-building activities

History and achievements

In 2009, at the Partners Conference in Toronto, Dominic Barton expressed his aspiration for each partner to connect more deeply to a network of leaders in the communities where they serve clients.

Develop partners who are “trusted peers” of influential leaders
Shape the critical issues faced by the world’s important institutions
Be called to participate in all circles that matter

To achieve this vision, Dominic started a firm initiative that has already generated significant impact. We have developed:

  • A vision and gold standard examples of what great looks like at the firm
  • Tools to measure impact
  • Gold standards and play books to develop best practice
  • A community of colleagues that can help
